Instuctions Voice Assistant

Wake up the voice assistant by saying its name or a wake-up phrase, such as "Hey Siri" or "OK Google".

Ask a question or make a request using natural language. For example, you might say "What's the weather like today?" or "Play some music by Taylor Swift."

Wait for the voice assistant to process your request and respond. The response may be spoken aloud or displayed on a screen, depending on the device you're using.

Follow up with additional questions or requests as needed. For example, you might say "What about tomorrow's weather?" or "Skip this song."

End the conversation by saying "thank you" or "goodbye" or simply stop speaking. Some voice assistants may automatically stop listening after a period of inactivity.
